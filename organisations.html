Organisations (8)

← Toggle

  • C-DAC

    Center for Advanced Computing (C-DAC), an autonomous institution operating under the Goverment of India institution, undertakes various multi-lingual computing projects and initiatives.

  • Indian Languages Corpora Initiative (ILCI)

    A project at the School of Sanskrit and Indic Studies, JNU, building parallel corpora for multiple Indian languages.

  • IndicNLP

    Open data platform and computing tools for the regional Indian languages.

    FOSS

  • KaGaPa

    Kannada Ganaka Parishat is an organisation that works on digital Kannada literature projects.

  • Linguistic Data Consortium For Indian Languages

    A distribution portal for text and speech corpora operated by the Government of India.

  • Localisation In Governance

    A Goverment of India Project that works on enabling Indic language localisation across government projects.

    FOSS

  • Kannada Sanchaya

    A non-profit organisation working on Kannada digitising projects.

    FOSS

  • Malayalam Swathanthra Malayalam Computing (SMC)

    A non-profit free software collective engaged in development, localization, standardization and popularization of various FOSS in Malayalam.
